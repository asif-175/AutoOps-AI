Q4. What is ITSM?
Answer

ITSM (IT Service Management) is the practice of designing, delivering, managing, and improving IT services.

Its objective is to ensure that IT supports business operations effectively.

Some important ITSM processes include:

Incident Management
Problem Management
Change Management
Request Management
Knowledge Management

Our project is mainly built around Incident Management.

Q5. What is an Incident?
Answer

An Incident is any unplanned interruption or degradation of an IT service that affects users.

Examples:

Laptop is slow
VPN is not working
Printer is offline
Outlook is not opening

The primary goal of Incident Management is to restore normal service as quickly as possible.
